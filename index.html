Admission Process

SIMS, Rohini, New Delhi, was established in 1999, as an affiliated Institute of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. The Institute runs BCA undergraduate course (1st and 2nd Shift) affiliated to GGSIPU as per the following details:

  • Duration of Course : 3 years
  • Mode : Full Time
  • Eligibility : 50% marks in 10+2 or equivalent in any discipline with Mathematics or Computer Science from a Board recognized by the Government of India.
  • Admission criteria: Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the GGSIP University, Delhi.
  • No. of Seats : 60+60
  • Management Quota Seats: The Institute conducts admission for 10% management seats.
